FAQ

Everything You Need to Know About Local SEO in the UK

Direct answers to the questions London business owners ask us most often — also the questions Google and AI tools like ChatGPT and Perplexity use to serve your customers.

What is local SEO and why does my London business need it? +
Local SEO is the process of optimising your online presence so your business appears in Google's local search results — including Google Maps, the Local Pack (the 3 businesses shown in a map above organic results), and local organic listings. For London businesses, local SEO is essential because 46% of all Google searches have local intent, and 76% of people who search locally visit a business within 24 hours. If you're not visible in local search, your competitors are taking those customers every single day.
How long does local SEO take to show results in London? +
Most London businesses see meaningful improvements within 3–4 months, with strong page 1 results appearing between months 4–6. Quick wins — like Google Business Profile optimisation and citation building — can show results within 4–8 weeks. The timeline depends on your industry competitiveness, your starting position, and the consistency of work applied. Our average client reaches page 1 for their core local keywords within 90 days.
How much does local SEO cost in the UK? +
Our local SEO packages start from £375 per month for the Starter plan (5 keywords, GBP optimisation, 25 citations). Our Growth plan is £750/month and our Domination plan is £975/month for larger or more competitive markets. The UK market median for local SEO is around £975/month. All packages include a free audit, no setup fees, and a rolling monthly contract — no long-term lock-in required.
Can you help my business rank in the Google Local Pack (Map Pack)? +
Yes — ranking in the Google Local Pack (the 3-business map box) is our primary goal for every client. We achieve this through Google Business Profile optimisation, NAP consistency across all directories, review generation, localised on-page content, and structured data markup. Businesses in the Local Pack receive 93% more actions (calls, directions, website visits) than those ranked below it.
What is the difference between local SEO and regular (national) SEO? +
National SEO aims to rank your website across the UK for broad, competitive keywords. Local SEO focuses on geographic searches — making your business visible to people searching in your specific city, borough, or postcode. Local SEO also includes Google Business Profile optimisation and Google Maps ranking, which national SEO doesn't address. For most London service businesses, local SEO delivers far better ROI because it targets high-intent, nearby customers who are ready to buy.
